Fam Mirza (born 26 June 1987) is a Serial Entrepreneur, investor, american marketer, and philanthropist. He is best known for creating and marketing consumer retail brands. Founder of the 1Face watch movement. A watch brand that partnered with NGOs to fund greenhouses, water wells, and treatment projects around the world.

Mirza expressed his entrepreneur passion at an early age selling his old toys around the neighborhood at the age of ten. Since than he has went on to create eight brands that have generated over 10MM in revenue.
